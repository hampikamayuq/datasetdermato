# Licensing and Ethics

Dermatology images can contain sensitive health information. Treat all images as potentially sensitive, even when they are publicly visible.

## DermNetNZ

DermNetNZ is an educational dermatology source. Public availability of a page does not automatically mean images can be redistributed in a dataset or used commercially. Before adding images to a redistributable package:

- review the current DermNetNZ terms of use
- record the license or permission status in `metadata/sources/source_catalog.csv`
- preserve attribution in `metadata/sources/dermnetnz_manifest.csv`
- avoid copying images into public releases until reuse is confirmed

## Dataset use

Recommended statuses:

- `pending`: terms not reviewed yet
- `approved`: reuse permitted under recorded conditions
- `restricted`: use allowed only for local research or with constraints
- `rejected`: do not use

## Privacy

Remove or reject images with:

- faces or unique tattoos not relevant to the lesion
- names, dates, record numbers, or watermarks containing personal data
- context that can identify a patient

## Medical disclaimer

This repository is for dataset organization and research workflows. It is not a medical device, diagnostic tool, or substitute for professional medical evaluation.
